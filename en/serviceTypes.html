Consultation

Our competent advice and advice is at your disposal prior to choosing and purchasing a safe. Of course, you can rely on them even afterwards.

Unfortunately, the current market is overwhelmed by cheap safes that do not provide the necessary reliable protection of your valuables money and documents. Outside, these safes can look pretty solid and massive, but when removing the rear door panel, you can see unreliable materials, mechanisms, and locks that are used to make them. These “safes” can literally be opened within minutes of low-skilled criminals using nothing more than a pair of ordinary tools. Owning such a safe is not a guarantee of your security, as the criminal is maximally facilitated with an easy-to-open “box”. Buying a safe is not only a matter of trust but also a long-term investment. Therefore, comprehensive prior consultation on existing options is not only your full right but also a necessary necessity.
