Vault Doors

The doors are designed and certified for bank vaults. They include multiple layers of different integrated materials, highly resistant against various attacks. Door model 320 achieved VII resistance category under EN 1143-1 for Secure storage of cash, valuables and data media. Dimensions and deployment details are defined for each site as part of the requirements definition and quotation process. In addition to the required locks the standard version includes an extra mechanical combination lock. Custom colors and additional protection can be offered.
