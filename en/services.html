Installation

Safe installation must be carried out by highly qualified specialists who are well aware of the risks that may occur in the process of moving and positioning the safe to the installation site.

For safes up to 700 kg, we recommend arranging a preliminary inspection by a company representative. For safes over 700kg, such consultation should be mandatory. This will help prevent inconveniences such as incorrect safe size, unforeseen presence of stairs, and other obstacles, which could lead to additional shipping costs.
